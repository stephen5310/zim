====== Jenkins Processes ======
Created mercredi 30 mars 2022

Go to ci.int.actility.com/job/GateAuto to see the operations we can do with Jenkins

===== LRR-Builder =====

* Click on the LRR-Builder folder
* Search for the type you want to build
* Click on Build with Parameters and choose the parameters
* Click on Build then and it will launch a build
* When the build is finished, go to repo.int.actility.com/ui/repos/tree/General/tarball/com/actility/jenkins/LRR-Builder to retrieve the artifacts

===== LRR-Full_Image_Packager =====

* Click on the LRR-Full_Image_Packager folder
* Click on the type you want to build
* Click on Build with Parameters and choose the parameters
* Click on Build then and it will launch a build
* When the build is finished, go to repo.int.actility.com/ui/repos/tree/General/tarball/com/actility/jenkins/LRR-Builder to retrieve the artifacts
